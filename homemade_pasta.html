Recipe for Homemade Pasta

Description

Craft your own fresh pasta at home with this straightforward recipe. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create delicious, tender noodles perfect for any sauce. This recipe yields about 1 pound of pasta, serving 4 people.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons water (as needed)

Instructions

  1. Combine flour and salt on a clean work surface or in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add beaten eggs.
  2. Gradually mix the flour into the eggs, forming a stiff dough. Add water as needed to achieve the right consistency.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth, about 3 to 4 minutes. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  4. Divide the dough into portions and roll out to desired thickness using a rolling pin or pasta machine. Cut into desired shapes.
  5. Cook the pasta in boiling salted water for 2 to 3 minutes until al dente. Drain and serve with your favorite sauce.
